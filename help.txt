
>>> This is a skill based game about betting and getting Ägg (:egg:)

Every now and then I will put an :egg: into your personal basket.
You can `/collect` these, and invest them in various ways.

**Available Commands**

_Player Specific_
* `/eggcollect` - Collects :egg: Ägg from your basket into your wallet
* `/eggroll [roll amount, random] [bet amount, a percentage (25%), all, random] [required]` - Start a new cool roll game. If `required` is defined, then no partial bets will be allowed.
* `/eggstats [@user/user]` - Show stats about yourself, or another player
* `/eggtop [page]` - Show top players, paginated
* `/eggeat [egg amount, defaults to 1, the recommended serving size]` - Eat eggs
* `/eggrecords` - List various Ägg world records.

`Ägg Roll v0.5`

__Please note that this is an alpha and your :egg:Ägg might get deleted__
