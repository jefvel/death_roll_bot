
>>> This is a skill based game about betting and getting Ägg (:egg:)

**Available Commands**

_Player Specific_
* `d.roll [roll amount] [bet amount]` - Start a new cool roll game
* `d.stats` - Show stats about yourself
* `d.top` - Show top 10 players

_Town (Server) Specific_
* `d.join` - Join the Town of the server. It is only possble to be a member of one town at a time.
* `d.contribute [Ägg amount]` - Contribute to the current server's town eggs (Klägg). (Multiples of 1000. 1000 Ägg = 1 Klägg)
* `d.serverstats` - Show info about the server.
